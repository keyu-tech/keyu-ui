04 — How we work

Four steps,
no surprises.

Engagements at Keyu follow a deliberate cadence. You'll always know what's happening this week and what's shipping next month.

01

Discover

A two-week embed with your team. We map the problem, agree the metric, and write a brief you sign off on.

02

Design

Architecture, data flow, interaction. Every decision documented; every assumption tested before code is written.

03

Build

Weekly demos, observable from day one, deployed to staging from week two. You see the work as it's being made.

04

Hand off

Docs, runbooks, a recorded walkthrough. Your team can run it. We're a Slack away if you want us closer.

Have something to unlock?

Tell us about your project. We respond within one business day with a brief, a price band, and an honest opinion on whether we're the right team.
